# Zomato Business Insights

## Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)

---

## Objective

The objective of this project is to perform Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) on the Zomato Restaurant Dataset to identify customer preferences, restaurant performance, pricing trends, online ordering services, and location-based patterns. The analysis aims to extract meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Dataset

The dataset contains information about restaurants listed on Zomato, including:

* Restaurant Name
* Restaurant Type
* Location
* Online Order Availability
* Table Booking Availability
* Customer Ratings
* Customer Votes
* Approximate Cost for Two
* Cuisines
* Popular Dishes
* Reviews

---

## Data Cleaning

The following preprocessing steps were performed:

* Missing values handled
* Duplicate records removed
* Data types corrected
* Outliers detected using Boxplots
* Outliers treated using the IQR Method

---

## Key Insights

* Restaurants with more customer votes generally have higher ratings.
* Online ordering is available for many restaurants, indicating strong customer demand.
* Restaurant popularity varies significantly by location.
* Most restaurants belong to the medium price range.
* Restaurant ratings remain high regardless of cost in many cases.
* Customer satisfaction is influenced by food quality and service.
* Restaurant type affects customer engagement.
* Data cleaning improved the quality and reliability of the analysis.

---

## Recommendations

* Improve food quality to increase customer ratings.
* Expand online ordering services.
* Focus marketing efforts on high-demand locations.
* Encourage customers to leave reviews.
* Maintain competitive pricing.
* Monitor customer feedback regularly.
* Use data-driven strategies for business expansion.

---

## Conclusion

The Zomato Business Insights project demonstrates how Exploratory Data Analysis can reveal valuable information about restaurant performance, customer behavior, pricing strategies, and business opportunities. The findings support informed decision-making and continuous business improvement.
